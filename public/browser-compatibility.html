Browser Compatibility

Our Free Invoice Generator is designed to work seamlessly across a wide range of modern web browsers. To ensure the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of your preferred browser. Below is a list of our supported browsers and tips for resolving common compatibility issues.

Supported Browsers

We officially support the following browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Version 80 and newer
  • Mozilla Firefox: Version 75 and newer
  • Microsoft Edge: Version 80 and newer
  • Safari: Version 13 and newer

While our invoice generator may function on older browser versions, we cannot guarantee full functionality or a seamless experience. We recommend updating your browser to one of the versions listed above.

Common Compatibility Issues

If you are experiencing issues with our invoice generator, it may be due to browser compatibility. Here are some common problems and their solutions:

  • PDFs Not Downloading: This can be caused by a pop-up blocker or an outdated browser. Please ensure pop-ups are enabled for our site and that your browser is up to date. For more detailed solutions, visit our PDF Download Problems page.
  • Layout Appears Broken: If the invoice generator does not look right, try clearing your browser's cache and cookies. An outdated cache can sometimes cause display issues.
  • Buttons Not Working: Non-responsive buttons can be a sign of a JavaScript error. Make sure JavaScript is enabled in your browser settings and that no extensions are interfering with our site.
